Personal Information

My name is Rashmi Chalise, a driven individual with a love of technology and a global outlook. My journey has taken me from the lovely location of Nawalparasi, Nepal, to the colorful and diverse continent of Australia, where I am currently studying a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the famous Wentworth Institute of Higher Education.

My interest in technology began at a young age, and I've been on a never-ending quest to learn more about it ever since. My scholastic experience in Australia not only extended my views, but also allowed me to immerse myself in a cosmopolitan atmosphere, cultivating a profound appreciation for variety and a global perspective.

I am more than a student in the field of information technology; I am an avid problem solver, creative thinker, and motivated learner. I thrive in the ever-changing world of information technology, continuously looking for new challenges and possibilities to develop unique solutions.

Aside from my academic endeavors, I am an adventurer at heart. Exploring new places, learning about diverse cultures, and meeting people from all walks of life energise me. I feel that the most important lessons in life are taught outside of the classroom, and I am excited to continue this rewarding path, both personally and professionally.
